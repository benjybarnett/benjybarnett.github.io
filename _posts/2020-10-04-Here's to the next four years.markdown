I don't suppose many people will check on my blog regularly, not least myself. But as I began my PhD last week, I thought it might be a good idea to have a place to chuck any thoughts I had on science, philosophy, life in academia, imposter syndrome, or anything else really. It is often hard to engage with papers, talks, etc. in real time - and I find that ideas and opinions often germinate hours or days later. Having this blog therefore gives me a medium on which to record these thoughts, rather than having them lost to the ether once the initial opportunity to discuss them has gone. 

I've also been hosting radio shows for the past 4 years across two radio stations: <a href="https://www.mixcloud.com/beat-roots/stream/">Beat Roots </a> on 1BTN in Brighton and the <a href="https://www.mixcloud.com/RadioFreeBrooklyn/playlists/non-resident-alien-show/">Non-Resident Alien Show</a> on Radio Free Brooklyn in New York. I reckon it's unlikely I'll have the time to commit to a regular show now I've started my PhD, so hopefully I'll be able to plug some of the tracks I've been listening to here, if anything to make sure I don't keep circling around the same five tunes on spotify forever and ever.
